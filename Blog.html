Fashion and Business

Published On: 01/11/2023

Zendaya is a successful model and fashion designer. She has modeled for major brands such as Beats Electronics, X-Out, Material Girl, CoverGirl, Chi Hair Care, Michael Kors, and Dolce & Gabbana. She has also released her own clothing line, Daya by Zendaya, and collaborated with Tommy Hilfiger on a capsule collection.

Zendaya's fashion shows are known for celebrating diversity and inclusivity, featuring women of color, plus-size models, and models aged up to 70.
